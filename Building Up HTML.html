How to Make a Peanut Butter Sandwich

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches are my favorite type of sandwich. Personally, I prefer crunchy peanut butter and grape jelly on whole white bread. However, this guide allows you to customize whatever you want in the sandwich.

  1. Gather the tools and ingredients.
    1. Knife
    2. Plate
    3. Your choice of bread
    4. Your choice of peanut butter
    5. Your choice of jelly
  2. Using the knife, spread the peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread.
  3. After cleaning the knife, spread the jelly on one side of another slice.
  4. Place the peanut buttered side and the jellyed side together.
  5. Put the sandwich on the plate.
  6. Eat and Enjoy!
